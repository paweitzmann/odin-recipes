Hummus

Description

My amazing and beautiful wife makes this hummus in large batchs every week. Makes 1 quart.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 lb dried chickpeas
  • 2 cups water
  • 1⁄4 tsp baking soda
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 Tbsp tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Sort and rinse chickpeas
  2. Simmer chickpeas in water with baking soda for 5 hours or until very soft.
  3. Drain chickpeas
  4. Crush and peel garlic
  5. In a food processor, combine cooked chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, garlic, and salt
  6. Blitz in food processor until smooth
