Black Beans

Description

This recipe is from Serious Eats. Goes great on tacos. Serves 10.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb black beans
  • 10 cups water
  • 1 yellow onion, quartered
  • 8 cloves garlic, peels and mashed through a garlic press
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1 orange

Steps

  1. Sort and rinse the beans.
  2. Combine beans, water, onion, garlic in a slow cooker. Reserve salt.
  3. Juice the orange into the slow cooker.
  4. Cut the rind into a few large chunks and add them to the slow cooker.
  5. Cook on high with lid stirring for about 4 hours or until the beans are almost done.
  6. Remove onion and orange rind from the cooker.
  7. Add salt and stir.
  8. Cook one more hour with lid off to allow liquid to reduce.
