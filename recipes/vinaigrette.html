Vinaigrette

Description

This salad dressing is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp olive brine
  • 1.5 Tbsp dijon mustard
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 0.25 cup EVOO
  • 0.25 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, soy sauce, brine, and mustard in a sizable jar with a tight fitting lid.
  2. Peel garlic and mash in a garlic press, then add it to the jar.
  3. Add EVOO and vegetable oil to the jar.
  4. Add black pepper.
  5. Place the lid tightly on the jar and shake well until the dressing is combined.
